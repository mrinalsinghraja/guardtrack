Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  GuardTrack Pro v1.0
Short version: GuardTrack Pro collects GPS location and camera photos during patrol. All data is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. No account required. No analytics. No ads.

1. Who We Are

GuardTrack Pro is developed by MSRX (Mrinal Singh Raja). If you have questions about this policy, contact us at mrinalsinghraja@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

DATA TYPE PURPOSE STORED WHERE
GPS location Tagged on patrol checkpoint photos as evidence of presence Device only
Camera photos (rear) Location evidence at each patrol checkpoint Device only
Camera photos (selfie) Guard identity verification at each checkpoint Device only
Patrol timestamps Record of when each checkpoint was completed Device only
Guard name & photo Embedded in patrol records and reports Device only
Site name & logo Embedded in patrol reports for identification Device only
Patrol report (WhatsApp) Shared to supervisor when user explicitly taps Share User-initiated only

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No analytics or crash reporting services
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No background location tracking
  • No automatic uploads to any server
  • No third-party SDKs that collect personal data

4. Permissions We Request

Camera — Required to capture patrol checkpoint photos and guard selfies. The app does not record video or audio.

Location (While Using) — Required to tag each checkpoint photo with GPS coordinates. The app only accesses location while actively in use. No background location tracking.

Photo Library — Optional. Requested only if you choose to upload a custom site logo. We read one image you select; we do not access your full library.

Microphone — The camera framework technically references microphone access. GuardTrack Pro does not record audio at any time.

5. Data Sharing

GuardTrack Pro does not share your data with any third party automatically or passively.

When you tap Share Report, the app generates a patrol report and opens WhatsApp with the content pre-filled. You control what is sent and to whom. This is entirely user-initiated.

We have no access to your WhatsApp messages or contacts.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored on your device using iOS standard storage (app documents directory and shared preferences). Data persists until you:

  • Start a New Shift (clears current patrol records)
  • Delete the app (iOS removes all associated data immediately)

We have no ability to access or delete data on your behalf — it is entirely under your control.

7. Children's Privacy

GuardTrack Pro is a professional tool intended for security personnel aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy when the app adds new features. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Your Rights

Since all data lives on your device and we have no server-side storage, you have full control at all times. You can delete all data by uninstalling the app. For any questions, contact us below.
